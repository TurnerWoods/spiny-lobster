What is the Cofounder Platform?

Cofounder is an AI agent orchestration system that enables businesses to deploy dozens of specialized AI agents working in parallel—coordinated by a "CEO" layer that manages priorities, dependencies, and quality control.

The Architecture

The platform operates on three levels:

  • CEO Layer: High-level strategy, prioritization, and decision-making
  • Management Layer: Task distribution, coordination, and quality gates
  • Agent Layer: 50+ specialized agents executing specific tasks in parallel

Ambassador: Spiny Lobster Creative

Maeve founded Spiny Lobster Creative to prove that a solo operator with AI agents can outperform traditional agencies with 50+ employees.

The Challenge

Traditional marketing agencies face a fundamental problem: talent is expensive, scaling is slow, and quality varies wildly between team members. Clients end up paying for agency overhead rather than results.

The Solution

Using the Cofounder platform, Spiny Lobster Creative operates with:

  • 1 human creative director (Maeve) for strategy and final approval
  • 50+ AI agents handling research, content, and optimization
  • Automated quality control and brand consistency checks
  • Real-time campaign monitoring and adjustment

"I'm essentially running a 50-person agency by myself. The agents handle the execution, I handle the strategy and client relationships. It's the future of how creative businesses will operate."
